# Publication Safety Notes

This repository is intended for recruiters and potential clients.

## What Is Safe To Publish

- high-level project summaries,
- skills demonstrated,
- public-safe architecture patterns,
- selected proof states,
- known limitations,
- screenshots without secrets,
- demo outputs with synthetic data,
- links to public repositories.

## What Must Stay Private

- source code from private/proprietary systems,
- credentials, tokens, keys, or secrets,
- raw customer or personal data,
- production infrastructure paths,
- exploit steps,
- adversarial bypass details beyond safe summaries,
- internal monetization strategy,
- unverified claims,
- sensitive system diagrams,
- anything that weakens Razorglint's product moat.

## Claim Safety Rule

Do not claim a system is production-ready, certified, compliant, breach-proof, or legally sufficient unless that exact claim is supported by reviewed evidence and allowed by the relevant project handbook.

Use language like:

- "prototype"
- "proof-of-concept"
- "local-first tool"
- "evidence-oriented"
- "tamper-evident design"
- "designed to support"
- "helps teams evaluate"

Avoid language like:

- "guarantees compliance"
- "prevents breaches"
- "fully secure"
- "AI Act compliant"
- "certified"
- "unhackable"

## Recruiter Repo Rule

This repo should be sharp and boring in the best way: clear, credible, and safe. No goblin cannon of twenty raw systems fired into a recruiter's face.
